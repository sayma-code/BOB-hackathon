including natural gas, coal, oil, and renewable energy sources, along with
information about operating companies and ownership structures.

## 7. Critical Issues and Findings

### 🔴 Major Problems:

1. **Foreign Control of Key Gas Production**
   - Chevron (USA) operates Bibiyana field, producing ~50% of Bangladesh's gas
   - Largest producing field controlled by foreign company

2. **Underdeveloped Domestic Resources**
   - 3+ billion tons of coal reserves undeveloped
   - Only 1 coal mine operational despite 5 discovered fields
   - Minimal oil production despite exploration

3. **Import Dependency**
   - Now importing LNG despite having gas reserves
   - 99%+ oil imported
   - Coal imported despite domestic reserves

4. **Depleting Gas Reserves**
   - Major fields like Titas are depleting
   - Production declining in older fields
   - New discoveries limited

5. **Limited Renewable Development**
   - Renewable energy <3% of energy mix
   - Solar and wind potential largely untapped
   - Heavy reliance on fossil fuels
