# Bangladesh Graduate Education & Employment Analysis

## Executive Summary

This analysis examines Bangladesh's graduate education system, employment outcomes, and opportunities for better earnings. With 363,000 annual graduates earning an average of $250/month starting salary, there is massive potential to increase earnings by $7.75 billion annually (306% increase) through upskilling, certifications, foreign employment, and entrepreneurship.

### Key Findings

- **Annual Graduates**: 363,000 (1.56M total tertiary students)
- **Employment Rate**: 63.8% (232K employed, 131K unemployed)
- **Underemployment**: 26.5% (61K underemployed)
- **Average Starting Salary**: $250/month (BDT 27,538)
- **Average 5-Year Salary**: $581/month (BDT 63,895)
- **Current Annual Earnings**: $2.53 billion
- **Potential Annual Earnings**: $10.28 billion
- **Increase Potential**: $7.75 billion (306%)
