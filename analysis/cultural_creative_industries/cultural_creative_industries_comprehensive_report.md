# Bangladesh Cultural & Creative Industries Export Potential

## Executive Summary

This analysis examines Bangladesh's cultural and creative industries potential for export revenue, focusing on the population remaining in Bangladesh (not migrating abroad). With 13.75 million people available for creative work, Bangladesh can grow cultural exports from $370 million to $6.95 billion annually.

### Key Findings

- **Available Workforce**: 13.75 million (youth + women)
- **Workers Needed**: 1.48 million (10.8% utilization)
- **Current Cultural Exports**: $370 million
- **Export Potential**: $6.95 billion
- **Increase**: $6.58 billion (1,778% growth)
- **Investment Required**: $1.03 billion
- **ROI**: 6.4x annually

**With proper implementation, Bangladesh can become a global cultural powerhouse, transforming its rich heritage into sustainable economic growth while empowering millions, especially women and youth.**
