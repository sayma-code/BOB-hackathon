# Bangladesh Manufactures Import - Local Production Potential Analysis

## Executive Summary

This analysis examines Bangladesh's manufactured goods imports and assesses the potential for local production. Out of the $40.56 billion total manufactures imports, we analyzed $29.70 billion across 23 key product categories.

### Key Findings

- **Current Imports Analyzed**: $29.70 billion (73% of total manufactures imports)
- **Local Production Potential**: $19.01 billion (64.0% reduction possible)
- **Investment Required**: $8.70 billion
- **Return on Investment**: 0.5 years (6 months payback period)
- **High Potential Items**: 9 products with ≥75% local production capability
