# Bangladesh Foreign Employment & Remittance Potential Analysis

## Executive Summary

This analysis examines Bangladesh's unemployment situation and the potential for sending workers abroad to generate remittance revenue. With 2.68 million unemployed and 10.38 million potential workers available, Bangladesh can significantly increase its remittance income from the current $21.5 billion to $30-34 billion annually.

### Key Findings

- **Current Situation**: 7.5 million Bangladeshis abroad sending $21.5 billion/year
- **Unemployment**: 2.68 million (3.63% rate) in 2024
- **Total Potential Workers**: 10.38 million available for foreign employment
- **Additional Potential**: 6.1 million workers can be sent abroad
- **Revenue Potential**: $5.25B to $12.24B additional remittance annually
