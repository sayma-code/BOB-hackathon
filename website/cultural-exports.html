Massive Untapped Potential

Bangladesh has 13.75 million people available for creative work (youth + women), but only needs 1.48 million (10.8% utilization) to achieve the $6.95B export target.

  • Size: 7.68 million

  • Focus: Digital creative services, modern industries

Implementation Strategy

1

Phase 1: Foundation (Years 1-2)

Establish Cultural Export Promotion Council
Launch "Bangladesh: Where Tradition Meets Innovation" brand
Build 20 Digital Creative Centers (capacity: 50K/year)
Establish 50 Traditional Craft Training Centers (capacity: 100K/year)
Create 10 Fashion Design Schools (capacity: 10K/year)
Launch Bangladesh Cultural Marketplace (e-commerce)
Participate in 20+ international exhibitions
Investment: $200M | Workers Trained: 200K | Export Increase: +$500M
2

Phase 2: Expansion (Years 3-5)

Scale to 60 Digital Centers, 150 Craft Centers, 30 Fashion Schools
Training capacity: 500K/year
Enter new markets: China, Japan, South Korea, Latin America
Develop premium positioning for luxury markets
Launch OTT content production and gaming studios
International marketing campaigns and brand building
Investment: $700M | Workers Employed: 800K | Export Increase: +$2.5B
3

Phase 3: Maturity (Years 6-10)

Full capacity: 1.48M workers employed
All product categories scaled to target levels
Global distribution networks established
Design innovation centers and R&D facilities
Eco-certification and fair trade practices
Global brand recognition achieved
Investment: $130M | Total Workers: 1.48M | Export Revenue: $6.95B
