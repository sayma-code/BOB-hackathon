Critical Issue

Bangladesh's tax-to-GDP ratio of 9.54% is one of the lowest globally, significantly below the sustainable target of 18% and far behind peer countries.

Why Tax Revenue is Low

Narrow Tax Base

  • Only 1.5M individual taxpayers (<1% of population)
  • Large informal economy (35-40% of GDP) untaxed
  • Agriculture sector largely exempt
  • Extensive tax exemptions and incentives
Loss: 3-4% of GDP

Tax Evasion & Avoidance

  • Under-reporting of income widespread
  • Cash-based economy avoids VAT
  • Transfer pricing by multinationals
  • Weak enforcement mechanisms
Loss: 3-5% of GDP

Weak Tax Administration

  • Manual, paper-based processes
  • Limited digitalization
  • Corruption in tax collection
  • Complex tax laws and procedures
  • Inadequate staff and resources
Loss: 1-2% of GDP

Customs Revenue Losses

  • Under-invoicing of imports
  • Smuggling and illegal trade
  • Excessive duty exemptions
  • Weak border controls
Loss: 1-2% of GDP

Revenue Enhancement Strategy

Target Achievement

Increase tax-to-GDP ratio from 9.54% to 18% over 5 years, generating an additional $35.22 billion annually.

Six Key Measures

1. Tax Administration Digitalization

HIGH PRIORITY Impact: $6.24B (1.5% of GDP) Timeline: Years 1-3

Key Actions:

  • Implement e-filing for all taxpayers
  • Digital payment systems
  • Automated tax assessment
  • Data analytics for compliance
  • Blockchain for land records

2. Broaden Tax Base

HIGH PRIORITY Impact: $10.41B (2.5% of GDP) Timeline: Years 1-4

Key Actions:

  • Formalize informal sector (target 50%)
  • Expand individual taxpayer base to 5M
  • Introduce minimum tax for businesses
  • Tax agricultural income above threshold
  • Simplify tax registration

3. Combat Tax Evasion

HIGH PRIORITY Impact: $8.33B (2.0% of GDP) Timeline: Years 2-5

Key Actions:

  • Strengthen audit capacity
  • Cross-border information exchange
  • Transfer pricing regulations
  • Penalties for non-compliance
  • Whistleblower protection

4. VAT System Reform

MEDIUM PRIORITY Impact: $6.24B (1.5% of GDP) Timeline: Years 1-3

Key Actions:

  • Reduce VAT exemptions
  • Implement VAT on digital services
  • Improve VAT refund system
  • Strengthen VAT compliance
  • Harmonize VAT rates

5. Property Tax Enhancement

MEDIUM PRIORITY Impact: $4.16B (1.0% of GDP) Timeline: Years 2-5

Key Actions:

  • Update property valuations
  • Digital property registry
  • Improve collection rates
  • Progressive property tax rates
  • Tax vacant land

6. Customs Modernization

MEDIUM PRIORITY Impact: $4.16B (1.0% of GDP) Timeline: Years 1-4

Key Actions:

  • Risk-based customs clearance
  • Electronic cargo tracking
  • Reduce duty exemptions
  • Combat smuggling
  • Trade facilitation
