Current Graduate Employment Situation

Critical Challenge

Bangladesh produces 363,000 graduates annually, but only 63.8% find employment, with 26.5% underemployed. Average starting salary is just $250/month.

Win-Win Outcome

This solution creates a virtuous cycle: better-educated, higher-earning graduates pay more taxes naturally (without rate increases), contributing to debt reduction while enjoying improved living standards. Unlike other solutions that require dedicated allocation, this generates automatic tax revenue as graduates prosper.
