Critical Resource Control Issue

Foreign Control of Key Resources

Bangladesh has significant natural resources, but 48.1% are under foreign control, resulting in $5.2 billion annual revenue loss. The largest gas field (Bibiyana) producing ~50% of Bangladesh's gas is operated by Chevron (USA).

Resource Reclamation Strategy

Target Achievement

Reclaim control of natural resources to save $5.2 billion annually through domestic production and reduced foreign profit-sharing.

1. Gas Field Nationalization & Development

Savings: $2.0B/year Investment: $1.5B Timeline: 5-7 years

Key Actions:

  • Renegotiate PSCs: Increase Bangladesh's share from current agreements
  • Gradual Takeover: As contracts expire, transition to Petrobangla operation
  • Capacity Building: Train Bangladeshi engineers and technicians
  • Technology Transfer: Require foreign operators to transfer knowledge
  • New Exploration: Invest in finding new gas fields domestically
  • Offshore Development: Develop Bay of Bengal resources

Target: Increase domestic control from 45% to 80% of production by 2035

2. Coal Field Development

Savings: $2.0B/year Investment: $800M Timeline: 5-10 years

Key Actions:

  • Develop Khalashpir: 1.3B tons - underground mining
  • Develop Dighipara: 750M tons - underground mining
  • Expand Barapukuria: Increase production capacity
  • Resolve Phulbari: Negotiate better terms or nationalize
  • Environmental Standards: Implement clean coal technology
  • Domestic Use: Reduce coal imports, save $2B/year

Target: Produce 50M tons/year domestically, eliminate coal imports

3. Renewable Energy Development

Savings: $1.0B/year Investment: $5B Timeline: 10 years

Key Actions:

  • Solar Power: 10,000 MW capacity (abundant sunshine)
  • Wind Power: 2,000 MW capacity (coastal areas)
  • Hydropower: Small-scale projects in hilly regions
  • Biomass: Agricultural waste to energy
  • Rooftop Solar: Incentivize residential and commercial
  • Grid Modernization: Smart grid for renewable integration

Target: 30% of electricity from renewables by 2035, reduce fossil fuel imports

4. Domestic Refining & Processing

Savings: $200M/year Investment: $200M Timeline: 3-5 years

Key Actions:

  • Build Refineries: Process crude oil domestically
  • Gas Processing: Upgrade gas processing facilities
  • Petrochemicals: Develop downstream industries
  • Value Addition: Create jobs and retain value
  • Technology: Modern, efficient processing
